SQL-92
5.2 <token> and <separator>
<newline> ::= !! implementation-defined end-of-line indicator

SQL-99
11) <newline> is the implementation-defined end-of-line indicator. 
NOTE 41   <newline> is typically represented by U+000A (  Line Feed  )

not required by ISO/IEC 9075.
