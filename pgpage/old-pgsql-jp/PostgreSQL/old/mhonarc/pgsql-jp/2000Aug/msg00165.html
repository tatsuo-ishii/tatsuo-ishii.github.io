>[Tue Aug  8 18:33:24 2000] [alert] [client 192.168.0.1] /home/test/
>public_html/.htaccess: Auth_PGpwd_whereclause takes one argument, an
> SQL fragement that canbe attached to the end of a where clause.

Auth_PGhost 127.0.0.1
Auth_PGport 5432
Auth_PGdatabase userauth

Auth_PGpwd_whereclause and ltime < datetime('now'::date)

Authtype Basic
<Limit POST GET>
require valid-user
</Limit>
