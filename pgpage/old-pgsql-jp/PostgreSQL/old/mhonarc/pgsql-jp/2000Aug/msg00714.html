       The Postmaster has informed me that some other backend died abnormally and possibly corrupted shared memory.
       I have rolled back the current transaction and am going to terminated your database system connection and exit.
       Please reconnect to the database system and repeat your query.
       pqFlush() -- backend closed the channel unexpectedly.
               This probably means the backend terminated abnormally before or while proccessing request.

というメッセージが出て切れてしまいます。バックエンドが異常終了している
