Yu Ooasa wrote:

> select distinct a.userid from
>   (select userid from enq_answer where qid=1 and answer='アメリカ') as a,
>   (select userid from enq_answer where qid=2 and answer in ('野球','サッカー')) as b,
>   (select userid from enq_answer where qid=3 and answer in ('国語','数学')) as c
> where a.userid=b.userid and a.userid=c.userid;
> 
> あんまりスマートじゃありませんね、他にあるかしら

最近話題のJOINを使うと（FULLじゃないけど）

select distinct userid from
enq_answer q1 join enq_answer q2 using(userid)
join enq_answer q3 using(userid)
where q1.qid=1 and q1.answer='アメリカ'
and q2.qid=2 and q2.answer in ('野球','サッカー')
and q3.qid=3 and q3.answer in ('国語','数学');
