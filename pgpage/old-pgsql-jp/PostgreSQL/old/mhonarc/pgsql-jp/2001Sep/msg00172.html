片松＠凸版 です

既に解決済みの様ですが、

Select id,name,mon,req.yen,pay.yen
From (req left join pay using(id,mon)) join mem using(id)
Where (id,mon) In (
    Select id,mon
    From req r2
    Group By id,mon
    Having sum(yen) != (Select sum(yen) From pay Where (id,mon) = (r2.id,r2.mon))
        or (Select sum(yen) From pay Where (id,mon) = (r2.id,r2.mon)) is null
);

これなら文言通りの様な気がします。
