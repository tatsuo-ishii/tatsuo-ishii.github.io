自己訂正

Select id,name,mon,req.yen,pay.yen
From (req left join pay using(id,mon)) join mem using(id)
          ^^^^
Where (id,mon) In (
    Select id,mon
    From req left join pay using(id,mon)
             ^^^^
    Group By id,mon,req.yen
                    ^^^^^^^
    Having req.yen != sum(pay.yen) or sum(pay.yen) is null
           ^^^^^^^                 ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
);

これでも、同じ月に同じ人で複数回
