>Postgres95 does not support any kind of column constraints.
>Unfortunately, this problem is not fixed in 0.03.  
>
>The real solution is to fix our still semi-broken rule systems, and then
>to implement constraints as rules.  (This is the approach taken by
>Illustra).   But right now, column constraints is not a high-priority
>enhancement or bug.   It may be a while before it shows up in
>postgres95.
