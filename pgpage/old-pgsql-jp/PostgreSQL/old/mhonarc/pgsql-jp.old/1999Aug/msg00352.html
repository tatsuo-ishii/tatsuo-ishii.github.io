[pgsql-jp 10198] PostgreSQL awards LinuxWorld '99 Editor's Choice

  • From: Tatsuo Ishii <t-ishii@sra.co.jp>
  • Date: Mon, 16 Aug 1999 11:07:20 +0900
石井です。PostgreSQL 6.5.1 が LinuxWorld '99 において、最優秀 RDBMS と
いうことで、Editor's Choice Award を受賞しました。詳細は
http://www.linuxworld.com/linuxworld/lw-1999-08/lw-08-penguin_1.html
にありますが、以下そこからの抜粋です。

PostgreSQL 6.5.1: A full-featured object-relational database
management system (DBMS), PostgreSQL has become one of
the most mature and powerful open source tools available. It is
quickly closing in on full ANSI 92 SQL compliance, and it has
a range of very advanced features such as r-tree indexing for
geographical data and concurrency control at a lower
granularity than common row-level locking. The latter feature
is unique among enterprise databases, including Oracle, DB2,
and Informix. 

The PostgreSQL team is very active, and there are constant
improvements, but every release is very stable. There is also
considerable third-party support, leading to bindings to C/C++,
Java, Python, Perl, PHP, Tcl, many query tools, and even some
support for Japanese Kanji. PostgreSQL is an excellent choice for
all but the most sophisticated enterprise uses. 

P.S.
http://linuxdev.net で行なわれていた「私の好きなDBMS」アンケートで
PostgreSQLが人気投票第1位になりました。
