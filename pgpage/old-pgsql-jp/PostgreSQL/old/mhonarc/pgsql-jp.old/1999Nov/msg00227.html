小竹です。こんにちは。ハードディスクをDMA転送対応にカーネル
をコンパイルしてまたまたbenchを行いました。（やりだすと癖に
なりそう(^^;)

cpu Pentium2 233MHz
mem 96M
HD IDE13G(DMA33)
FreeBSD 3.3RELEASE、PostgreSQL6.5.2

◎su-2.03$ bin/postmaster -S -i -D/db/data -o '-S 1024'

su-2.03$ pgbench-1.1/pgbench bench
tps = 11.233670(including connections establishing)
tps = 11.694539(excluding connections establishing)

su-2.03$ pgbench-1.1/pgbench -c 4 -t 100 bench
tps = 10.279139(including connections establishing)
tps = 10.306335(excluding connections establishing)


◎su-2.03$ bin/postmaster -S -i -D/db/data -o '-S 1024 -F'

su-2.03$ pgbench-1.1/pgbench -c 4 -t 100 bench
tps = 58.180210(including connections establishing)
tps = 58.970053(excluding connections establishing)

su-2.03$ pgbench-1.1/pgbench bench
tps = 56.817536(including connections establishing)
tps = 68.882858(excluding connections establishing)

でした。
